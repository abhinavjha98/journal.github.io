Aims and Scope:

 

The Journal of Innovation in Engineering & Applied Science (JIEAS) is an open access peer reviewed journal that is committed to timely publication of original research, surveying and tutorial contributions on the analysis and development of computing, information, communication and applied science.

The journal is designed to serve research community, graduate students and others interested in state-of-the art research activities in Engineering & Applied Science. Researchers are assumed to have a common interest in information science, but with diverse backgrounds in the field of Engineering & Applied Science. The journal publishes high-quality, refereed articles. It emphasizes a balanced coverage of both theory and its relevance.

The journal focuses primarily on all fields of engineering and applied science. Manuscripts are invited from academicians, researchers and students for publication consideration. Papers are accepted for editorial consideration through email jieaseditors@gmail.com with the understanding that they have not been published, submitted or accepted for publication elsewhere.

JIEAS publishes 2 issues per year. Published papers will be available on line (free access)
